A new website is like a new chapter.

I'm Max. I love so many different things, but most of all I love this life. I wanted a place to write, so I built one. I feel like it's my own unique corner of the internet.

The site runs on [Astro](https://astro.build/), a framework that I value for its prioritization of content, simplicity, and good web fundamentals. My previous site used [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/), and it'll always hold a special place in my heart for containing some of the first lines of [React](https://react.dev/) code I ever wrote.

That old site shipped 259 KB of JavaScript to load one page. This one ships 14 KB total, for the whole site. That's an 18x reduction, and I see this as doing my part to conserve our planet's valuable natural resources.

I also just got a [NuPhy Air75 v2](https://nuphy.com/products/air75-v2) with Cowberry switches, so every keystroke here sounds delightful. It's a fresh experience with a drastically different form from my daily driver [Kinesis Advantage 360 Pro](https://kinesis-ergo.com/keyboards/advantage360/) (with blank keycaps, by the way).

More posts, more projects, and more fun stuff coming soon. Thanks for stopping by.

-Max
